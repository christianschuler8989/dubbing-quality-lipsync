Halli, Hallo and a shiny morning to you all,

for my bachelors thesis I am working on the perception of lip-synchrony in videos,
which naturally demands a study with human participants.

Luckily no one has to leave their comfy home for this and everything happens online! :)
For this every participants watches a few short (~3 sec.) videoclips and 
grades them in correspondance to the unmodified original version.

The duration for this run of the study is supposed to be about 10 minutes
and if every one would just do a hand full of tests,
it would result in a lot of usefull data for the analysis :D
Don't be alarmed: the study presents up to 25 tests,
but once the concentration falters, it is encouraged to
take a break or just end the study at any time! ;)
